About

I am a Member of Technical Staff at xAI, currently on the Science of RL team. I also work on post-training and reasoning.

My contributions since May 2025:

  • Grok 4.1 Co-creator, led post-training RL training and recipe studies.
  • Grok 4.1 Fast Co-creator, led post-training RL training and recipe studies.
  • Grok 4 Fast Co-creator, led post-training RL training and recipe studies, co-led distillation and evals.
  • Grok 4 Core contributor, synthetic datasets, evals, tool use.

Background

Previously, I was an EECS undergraduate at UC Berkeley (2021-2025), where I was fortunate to be advised by Ion Stoica and built LMArena.

During my undergrad, I also spent a year full-time at Nexusflow as part of the LLM post-training team, working with Banghua Zhu and Jiantao Jiao. Additionally, I briefly interned as a student researcher at Google AI Research, where I was working on reasoning.

Research Interests

I am very interested in fundamental problems in training large models, building more capable and reliable models, and solving superintelligence.

Some problems I've been thinking about:

  1. How can we transfer intelligence learned in verifiable domains to open-ended questions?
  2. Design and formulate less hackable and more interpretable reward signals.
  3. How can we leverage multi-agents to train smarter models?
  4. How to train faster, more efficient reasoning models / agents?

I am actively thinking about new training paradigms.
